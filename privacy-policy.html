Privacy Policy

Flight Booking Agent API

Effective Date: January 15, 2025

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Flight Booking Agent API ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our flight booking services through ChatGPT Actions or other integrations.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Flight Search Queries: Origin, destination, travel dates, passenger count, and travel class preferences
  • Booking Information: Passenger details (names, dates of birth, passport information), contact information (email, phone), and address details
  • User Preferences: Travel style preferences, airline preferences, and other customization settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: API request logs, response times, and error tracking
  • Technical Information: Request IDs, timestamps, and deployment environment data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process flight search requests and provide real-time flight options
  • Complete flight bookings through our Amadeus API integration
  • Personalize your experience based on travel preferences
  • Improve our services and troubleshoot technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations and maintain service security

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Flight Booking: With Amadeus API to complete your flight reservations
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate our API (e.g., Vercel for hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • OAuth 2.0 authentication for secure access
  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS
  • Secure API key management
  • Regular security audits and updates

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and complete your bookings
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Flight booking data is typically retained for 7 years to comply with travel industry regulations.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of certain data collection
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • Amadeus API: For real-time flight search and booking services
  • ChatGPT Actions: For AI-powered flight search and booking
  • Vercel: For secure hosting and deployment

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review them.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during such transfers.

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@porter-preview.vercel.app
  • API Endpoint: https://porter-preview.vercel.app/api/health
  • OpenAPI Documentation: https://porter-preview.vercel.app/openapi.json

13. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Industry-standard privacy practices for travel services
